Tom Yum Pasta

Description

We love experimenting with flavours and concocted not one, but three different recipes for your Asian fusion pasta cravings.

Ingredients

  • 8 Prawns
  • 1 Lemongrass
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • 2 tbsp Tom Yum Paste
  • 170g Spaghetti

Steps

  1. In a pot of boiling water, cook 170g of Spaghetti according to pack instructions.
  2. In a pan on medium low heat, add in 2 tbsp of cooking oil. Add in minced garlic. Saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add in Tom yum paste and water. Mix well until sauce forms.
  4. Add in prawns and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add in cooked pasta, and 1 small ladle of pasta water.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Mix, coat sauce evenly and serve. Garnish with sliced kaffic lime leaves.
